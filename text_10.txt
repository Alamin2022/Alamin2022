Uzbekistan ratifies an international agreement in statistics field 
The presidential resolution “On accession to an International Treaty” was adopted.
According to the resolution, Uzbekistan has joined the agreement on cooperation in the field of official statistics.
The agreement was signed on May 31, 2013 in Minsk at a meeting of the Council of Heads of Government of the Commonwealth of Independent States.
The State Statistics Committee has been designated as the competent authority responsible for the implementation of this international agreement.
The Cabinet of Ministers and the heads of relevant ministries and departments will ensure the fulfillment of Uzbekistan’s obligations after the entry into force of this international agreement.
